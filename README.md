## Key Features

* **Realistic Skin Physics:** The core of Jenny Mod's appeal lies in its incredibly realistic skin physics.  Each player's skin reacts to their actions in a nuanced way, creating a truly immersive experience.  This isn't just a simple "stick to the ground" effect; it’s a dynamic and responsive system.

* **Dynamic Movement:** Players move with a surprising amount of realism.  The system considers factors like momentum, gravity, and even slight wobbles, leading to incredibly fluid and believable character animations.

* **Body Tracking:**  The mod meticulously tracks the player's body and its orientation, adding a layer of visual feedback that is surprisingly engaging.  It's not just about standing still; players can subtly shift their weight and posture.

* **Facial Recognition:**  The mod has a rudimentary facial recognition system that detects player faces, triggering specific animations and visual effects when a player is near.

* **Sound Effects:**  Adding to the immersion, the mod features subtle and effective sound effects that complement the movement and appearance, enhancing the experience even further.

* **Variety of Animations:** A huge range of animations are available, from simple walking and jumping to complex poses and gestures.

* **Customizable Skins:** Players can drastically change their skins, drastically altering the visual experience and influencing the movement of other players.

## Gameplay & Customization

* **Skin Switching:**  Players can switch between different skins using the menu options.  The mod’s skin library is extensive, offering a wide variety of styles and aesthetics.
* **Skin Settings:**  Within the mod's options menu, you can customize various settings such as:
    * **Sensitivity:**  Adjusts how quickly the skin responds to movement.
    * **Speed:**  Controls the speed at which the skin moves.
    * **Weight:**  Determines how much weight is applied to the skin.
    * **Rotation:**  Controls the rotation of the skin.
* **Skin Customization:**  Players can add and remove skins to create unique and personalized experiences.
* **Multiplayer Mode:**  Jenny Mod is exceptionally effective in multiplayer environments, allowing for highly visible and engaging movement displays.

## Challenges & Considerations

* **Performance:**  Jenny Mod can be demanding on hardware, particularly with complex skins and high sensitivity settings.  Optimize your settings for smooth performance.
* **Complexity:**  The mod's features are numerous, and understanding all the settings and options can take time to master.
* **Compatibility:**  While generally compatible with most versions of Minecraft Java Edition, some older versions may require additional configuration.
